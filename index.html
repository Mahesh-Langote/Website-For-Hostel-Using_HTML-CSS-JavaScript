About Hostel

Sangli, the largest city of Maharashtra has been developed into an educational hub in last few years.Thousands of students not only from Maharashtra, but from across the nation comes forward to study and to engage in an effort to complete the dreams of their bright future. The first and foremost requirement of students who come from different places from outside the city is "Safe and convenient residence away from homes", where they can spend and dedicate their precious time for studies without any hurdle. Keeping in mind the needs of students coming for higher education at Sangli, establishment of Chintamanrao Managment Pvt Ltd Boys Hostel was taken.

Full-fledged hostel facilities for boys students have been located in Indore to cater to the needs of outstation students coming for higher studies.
