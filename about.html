A B Maheshwari Educational Trust,Indore was created with the inspiration of Pujya Swami Satyamitranand Giriji , Rashtriya Sant Swami Govind Dev Giriji and ABM Mahasabha Keeping in mind the needs of students coming for higher education at Indore. Principle Donor Jaju Family and other many members of Maheshwari Society from all over the country have played pivotal role in this noble cause of establishing the Hostel.

About Hostel
The first and foremost requirement of students who come from different places from outside the city is "Safe and convenient residence away from homes", where they can spend and dedicate their precious time for studies without any hurdle.

The hostel is constructed on nearly 14000 square feet of the land. The foundation of the hostel was laid by Rashtriya Sant Swami Govind Dev Giriji and other eminent personalities of Maheshwari Samaj.

The construction of Hostel Building was completed in a very short span of time by the tireless and relentless hard work, foresight and diligence of all dedicated office bearers, donors. The Hostel Building was inaugurated on 7th August 2016 in the glorious presence of Padhma Bhushan Smt. Rajshree ji Birla other dignitaries of Maheshwari and other Society
